About

Megan

Joe

Delaney

Dancing, Binky, Elmo.

Joe will sometimes tell the story of the night they met from his perspective; he tells his audience that he immediately was captivated by the girl his friend introduced him to and even said to 'Megan seems like wife material'. Although Megan questions the truth in his story it makes her blush every time she hears it. (She then wonders why it took him so long to ask her out...)

After Joe mustered the courage to ask Megan to go steady their relationship blossomed and it wasn't very long until they were very serious about each other. Their love brought them the best of surprises not but a year later, when they found out they would be parents. Joe quickly found out Megan was not only 'wife material' but also 'mommy material' and they welcomed Delaney Elizabeth into their world on November 3rd 2013.

Their first year as parents was a whirl wind full of many wonderful memories and even more learning experiences. That year their love for one another only grew stronger and Joe decided to pop the question on a family trip to the 1000 Islands. On August 29th, 2014, after walking through the halls and gardens of Boldt Castle a place built by a loving husband for his wife in the early 1900s Joe asked Megan to be his wife.
