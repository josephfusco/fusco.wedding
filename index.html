Megan met Joe while attending college at the State University of New York at Brockport. He was the 'handsome' bouncer at The Stoneyard Bar & Grill; Megan and her friends favorite bar. If you know Megan, you know she is very out-going and she didn't hesitate to use her quirky charm on Joe when they were first introduced by mutual friends. To her dismay Joe did not seem interested, and months went by all the while Megan would always look forward to saying Hi to Joe while out. They would share conversations here and there on the bar's patio but all of them were awkward and none them ended with Joe asking for Megan's number. It wasn't until Joe's friend and Stoneyard's bartender at the time, Shane, (The Best Man) stepped in. Joe had been 'crushing' on Megan since he met her but it was his reserved nature that made it difficult for him to say the words 'may I have your number?'
